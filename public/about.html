Our mission is to digitize the global farm.

Founded in 2022, FarmTrack was built by farmers, for farmers. We believe that technology should be as grounded as the earth we work on.

The FarmTrack Story

It started with a simple problem: lost paperwork and missed vaccinations. Our founder, a third-generation rancher, realized that the traditional way of keeping records was holding farmers back from their true potential.

Today, FarmTrack helps thousands of farmers across 15 countries secure their livestock's data, ensuring better health outcomes and verifiable ownership for generations to come.
