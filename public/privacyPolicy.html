Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Introduction

At FarmTrack, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users' livestock data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information within our digital registry.

Data Collection

We collect information necessary to maintain a reliable livestock management system:

  • Farmer profile information (Name, Contact, Location).
  • Livestock identification (Tag IDs, Breed, Age, Gender).
  • Health records (Vaccination history, Medical treatments).
  • Ownership transaction history for verification.

Data Security

Data security is our top priority. We implement the following measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmissions.
  • Secure cloud storage with automated daily backups.
  • Role-based access control to ensure only authorized users view sensitive data.
  • Compliance with national agricultural data protection standards.

Your Rights

As a user of FarmTrack, you have the right to:

  • Access and download your complete livestock registry records.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate data.
  • Delete your profile and associated data (subject to legal registry requirements).
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
